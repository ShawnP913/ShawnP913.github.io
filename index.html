Kendall Ducks Basketball Analysis

Shawn

Shawn is a versatile guard that can play off the ball or create his own shot. He has a strong mid range and a developing 3 point shot and is known as one of the best defensive players in the Kendall Parks League.

Conclusion: Do not sleep on Shawn's offensive power because he will shoot the lights out in the midrange or find an open player for the pass, you do not want Shawn guarding you because it will be a tough game, and don't sleep on his offensive power because he will shoot the ball.

Kadrian

Kadrian is a defensive stopper with an incredible post game built on fundamentals. Although he may not be the best at handling the ball, make no mistake that this young buck will drive the ball and lose you in the post making him a threat anywhere inside the key.

Conclusion: Kadrian is a high iq player that thrives in the post, he will back you up and shoot his signature unblockable post fadeaway.

Marcos

Marcos is a shifty guard that will blow by you on the court and use all sorts of crafty layups to finish strong. Marcos has also shot consistently from 3 this year, showing signs of a newly developed skill to add to his arsenal making him a threat anywhere on the court.
